### 1. MixedUse-8Storey-2M-v2 (2025-01-19) [ARCHIVED - v2 Methodology]

**Requirements:**
- ¬£2M profit from ¬£200k capital (10x ROI)
- 8-storey mixed-use development opportunities (Class E + C3)
- High precision, 10-50 properties per filter

**Iterations:** 3 (Converged)

**Final Filters:**
1. Tall Building Zone Option Agreements (10-20 properties expected)
2. Expired 8-Storey Planning + Market Motivation (5-12 properties expected)
3. Airspace-Ready Option Agreements (15-25 properties expected)

**Key Learnings (Design Phase):**
- Height calculations must match storey requirements (25m+ for 8 storeys)
- ¬£200k requires option agreements, not direct purchase
- Policy support filters (tall building zones, PTAL, HDT) essential
- Motivation signals must be specific and searchable
- Minimum 2 iterations prevented major errors from reaching production

**Key Learnings (Implementation Phase):**
- **Pattern #6:** LLMs assume filters exist that don't (no "Expired planning status")
- **Pattern #7:** Prose format doesn't work - structured format does! (CRITICAL)
- Units are acres/sqft not sqmt/sqm (must convert)
- Tested diagnostic filter: 10 results in Slough ‚úÖ
- SearchLand AI parses structured format perfectly

**Convergence:** Achieved after 3 design iterations + 1 implementation iteration

**Status:** ‚úÖ Implementation validated (10 real results) - **ARCHIVED (v2 methodology obsolete)**

**Location:** `projects/archive/MixedUse-8Storey-2M-v2-convergent-20251119/`

**Note:** This project used the old convergent methodology (v2). For current best practices, see v3 projects.

**Reference For:**
- Mixed-use residential + commercial projects

- Tall building/high-density searches
- Limited capital (<¬£500k) creative structuring
