## NEW PATTERN: Nutrient Neutrality Offset Banking

**Category:** Regulation-Driven / Environmental Mitigation  

**Strategic Rationale:** LPAs inside Solent, Norfolk, and Tees catchments need off-site nutrient credits before granting resi permissions. Buying or optioning Grade 3b/4 cereal land in the designated zones lets you sell offset capacity to housebuilders who are otherwise stuck.  

**Key Filters:**  
1. Ownership: Freehold, private/company (`SearchlandContext/PresetFiltersBuiltIntoSearchLand.md:438-447`).  
2. Site size: >80,000 sq m (enough for meaningful mitigation) (`SearchlandContext/PresetFiltersBuiltIntoSearchLand.md:443-444`).  
3. Habitat: Cereal crops (high phosphate output to convert) (`SearchlandContext/PresetFiltersBuiltIntoSearchLand.md:445-446`).  
4. Constraint: Nutrient neutrality area = TRUE.  
5. Optional: Within 20 km of growth settlements to ease transactional logistics.
