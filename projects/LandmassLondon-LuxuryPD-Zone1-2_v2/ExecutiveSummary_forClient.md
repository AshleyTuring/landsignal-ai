## Appendix: Key Terms

**Permitted Development (PD):** Development rights that don't require full planning permission, only prior approval (56 days). Includes Class MA (commercial-to-residential), airspace, and Class G (above shop conversions).

**Class MA:** Specific PD right allowing conversion of commercial buildings (Class E - offices, retail, etc.) to residential use without full planning permission.

**GDV (Gross Development Value):** Total sales value of completed development before deducting costs.

**ROI (Return on Investment):** Profit divided by total investment, expressed as percentage.

**MEES (Minimum Energy Efficiency Standards):** Regulation preventing rental of properties with EPC ratings below E (F and G rated properties cannot be legally rented).

**EPC Rating:** Energy Performance Certificate rating (A-G scale, A being most efficient).

**Liquidation:** Legal process where a company's assets are sold to pay creditors. Liquidators have legal obligation to sell, creating timeline pressure and negotiation leverage for buyers.

**Prior Approval:** Fast-track planning process for Permitted Development (56-day statutory timeline vs. 8-12 months for full planning).
