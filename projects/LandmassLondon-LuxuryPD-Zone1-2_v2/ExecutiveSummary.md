## Approach A: Class MA Liquidation Plays (Comprehensive)


**Status:** ‚úÖ VALID  
**Original LLM:** Claude  
**Pattern:** Class MA PD + Forced Sale Motivation  
**Capital Required:** ¬£12M-¬£68M (acquisition ¬£10M-¬£60M + refurb ¬£2M-¬£8M)  
**Expected Results:** 5-15 properties (highly targeted Zone 1-2)

## **SearchLand AI Prompt (Copy-Paste Ready)**

**START WITH PRESET:** #32 "Class MA eligible commercial sites"

**THEN ADD THESE REFINEMENT FILTERS:**
```
1. Sales listing price: is greater than 5000000 AND less than 60000000
2. Company status: is one of [Liquidation, In Administration]
3. Property floor area (sqft): is greater than 10000 AND less than 100000
4. Tenure: is one of [Freehold]
5. Planning constraints: does not contain [Green Belt, Flood Zone 3, SSSI, Ancient Woodland]
```

## **Rationale**
Combines the strongest possible seller motivation (liquidation = legally obligated to sell) with the lowest-risk development route (Class MA permitted development). Liquidators prioritize speed and certainty over price, creating 20-30% discount potential. Freehold tenure eliminates leaseholder complications in PD conversions. Large buildings (10,000-100,000 sqft) deliver scale for luxury residential (15-150 flats). Price band targets Zone 1-2 commercial stock.

**Critique Integration:** Removed EPC filter (liquidation motivation sufficient); added planning constraints exclusion for clean PD path; freehold filter ensures title simplicity (Pattern 23A).

## **Strengths**
- **Maximum motivation:** Forced sale creates non-negotiable timeline pressure
- **Proven route:** Class MA is established PD path (lower planning risk)
- **Professional sellers:** Liquidators move quickly, no emotional negotiation
- **Clean titles:** Freehold filter reduces assembly risk
- **Scale advantage:** Large buildings suit Landmass's refurbishment expertise

## **Limitations**
- **Low volume:** Liquidation stock is rare (5-15 expected results)
- **As-is condition:** Deferred maintenance likely, requires thorough survey
- **Quick decisions:** Liquidators may run 30-90 day sale processes
- **Data gaps:** Liquidation properties may have incomplete marketing info

## **Implementation Strategy**

1. **Search execution:** Run preset with filters in Zone 1 LPAs (Westminster, City, Camden, K&C) and prime Zone 2 (Tower Hamlets, Southwark)
2. **Immediate triage:** Export all results, prioritize by location and vacancy status
3. **Liquidator contact:** Direct approach within 48 hours (speed advantage over competition)
4. **Desktop analysis:** Planning history check, structural desktop review (¬£5k-¬£10k)
5. **Offer strategy:** 15-25% below market value (factor in as-is risk + liquidator timeline pressure)
6. **Due diligence:** Structural survey, M&E assessment, title review (2-3 weeks)
7. **Prior approval:** Class MA application (56 days statutory timeline)
8. **Refurbishment:** Luxury fit-out (¬£150-¬£250/sqft for Zone 1, ¬£100-¬£180/sqft Zone 2)
9. **Exit:** Sell at ¬£2,000-¬£4,000/sqft (Zone 1) or ¬£1,000-¬£2,000/sqft (Zone 2)

## **Capital Breakdown**
- **Acquisition:** ¬£10M-¬£60M (20-25% discount from market = ¬£2.5M-¬£15M margin creation)
- **Refurbishment:** ¬£2M-¬£8M (luxury fit-out for 20-100 units)
