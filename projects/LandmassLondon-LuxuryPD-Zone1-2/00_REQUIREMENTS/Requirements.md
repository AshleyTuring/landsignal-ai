### Top Motivation Signals:

- Filter: `EPC rating: is one of [E, F, G]`

**3. Lease Expiry (Income Cliff)**
- Filter: `Lease end date: is before [date]`
- Issue: Rental income stops, void property costs
- Seller motivation: Sell now (tenanted) vs face vacancy
- Discount: 10-20% below market

**4. Long-Term Institutional Holds**
- Filter: `Latest sold date: is before 2015-01-01` (10+ years)
- Ownership: Institutional, corporate (non-core asset)
- Seller motivation: Portfolio rationalization, trapped equity
- Discount: 5-15% below market (less urgent than liquidation)

**5. Expired Planning Permission**
- Filter: Check planning history, look for lapsed consents
- Issue: Sunk costs, planning re-application required
- Seller motivation: Exit vs re-invest in planning
- Discount: 10-25% (depending on viability)
