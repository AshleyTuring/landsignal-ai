## Rationale
This approach targets companies in financial distress that own small commercial properties. The regulatory environment creates opportunities for motivated sellers seeking to crystallize assets during insolvency proceedings.

## Strengths
- Highly motivated sellers due to financial distress
- Clear title through insolvency process
- Lower purchase prices due to distressed circumstances
- Established legal framework for asset sales

## Limitations
- Properties may require significant refurbishment
- Company ownership can complicate transactions
- Competition from other creditors in insolvency

## Implementation Strategy
1. Identify distressed companies through Companies House
2. Contact administrators/liquidators
3. Conduct due diligence on property portfolios
4. Negotiate direct asset purchases
