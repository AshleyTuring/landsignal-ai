## LESSONS LEARNED

### What Works:
- Private owners (not companies)
- Long hold periods (15+ years)
- Expired or expiring EPCs (double pressure)
- 100% unconstrained properties (clean)
- Single address (one decision maker)

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- Properties already on market
- Corporate ownership
- Recent purchases (<10 years)
- Multiple addresses (fragmented ownership)
- HMO data inconsistencies (research further)
